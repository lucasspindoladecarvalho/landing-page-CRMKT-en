Who is Catherine Ribeiro

Overview

Catherine Ribeiro is a passionate and experienced digital strategist, specializing in creating authentic and personalized marketing strategies for specialists. With a journey marked by constant improvement and innovation, Catherine has a natural talent for connecting brands with their target audience in a unique and impactful way.

Education and Career

Catherine's journey began with her biomedical education in Brasília. Despite starting her academic career in healthcare, Catherine always had an entrepreneurial spirit. From a young age, she was influenced by her entrepreneurial family and the digital world, developing skills in customer service and online interactions.

Start in Digital Marketing

In 2019, Catherine co-founded Fundamental Sunflower, an experience that reinforced her passion for digital marketing. Even with the end of the partnership, her desire to pursue a career as a digital strategist remained strong. She decided to focus on her childhood passions and redirect her career to marketing, where she could explore her creativity and strategic skills.

Professional Development

Catherine pursued continuous improvement in personal development and marketing, specializing in neuromarketing and neurosales. In 2020, during the pandemic, she faced new personal and professional challenges but continued to innovate. Her passion for gaming and experience with burnout led her to create GameHealer, a company focused on helping streamers and e-sports teams grow on social media.

New Projects and Changes

New Projects and Changes In 2023, Catherine founded Mentoria A Nerd Rica, aiming to bring authenticity to women gamers. Her marriage marked a new stage in her personal and professional life, with her husband becoming a traffic management specialist and collaborating on projects. In 2024, she started a Marketing for Games course at EBAC and planned her move to Texas, where she serves as an International Business Advisor and SaaS Media.

Mission and Values

Catherine Ribeiro believes in the power of uniqueness and authenticity. She is dedicated to developing creative and personalized strategies that reflect each client's unique essence. Her mission is to break the cycle of "more of the same" in digital marketing, helping specialists build their online authority authentically and distinctly.

Contribution and Impact

At Catherine Ribeiro Marketing, Catherine leads a team of specialists dedicated to transforming the digital presence of their clients. Her customer-centric approach and ability to create impactful content make her a reference in the industry. Catherine is committed to helping her clients achieve their goals and turn their visions into reality.
